# The Warfare Model

[Back to Home](../)

## Key Question

*How do I stand firm against evil and fight the good fight of faith?*

## Overview

The Warfare Model recognizes the spiritual battle and calls us to make choices that resist evil and advance God's kingdom. We are not passive observers but active participants in a cosmic conflict between good and evil.

## Discussion Questions

1. How does recognizing spiritual warfare change how we approach decisions?
2. What does it mean to "put on the full armor of God"?
3. How do we fight without becoming anxious or paranoid?

## Key Takeaways

- The Christian life involves real spiritual conflict
- Our choices have kingdom significance
- Victory is assured in Christ, but vigilance is required
