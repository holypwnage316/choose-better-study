# The Wisdom Model

[Back to Home](../)

## Key Question

*What is the wise thing to do in this situation?*

## Overview

The Wisdom Model applies prudence and discernment to navigate complex situations where simple commands may not directly apply. Life presents countless decisions where no explicit command exists, and wisdom helps us choose the better path.

## Discussion Questions

1. What kinds of decisions require wisdom rather than a direct command?
2. How do we grow in wisdom?
3. What's the difference between wisdom and personal preference?

## Key Takeaways

- Many decisions require wisdom, not just rule-following
- Wisdom comes from fearing the Lord and seeking counsel
- Proverbs and Ecclesiastes train us in wise thinking
