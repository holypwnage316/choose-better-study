# The Imitation Model

[Back to Home](../)

## Key Question

*What would Jesus do? What examples has God given us to follow?*

## Overview

The Imitation Model calls us to follow the example of Christ and other godly individuals in Scripture. We look at how Jesus lived, how Paul conducted his ministry, and how the heroes of faith responded to challenges.

## Discussion Questions

1. What are the strengths of asking "What would Jesus do?"
2. What are the limitations of this approach?
3. Can you think of a situation where imitating Christ's example gave you clear guidance?

## Key Takeaways

- Christ is our ultimate example
- Scripture provides many models of faithful living
- Imitation requires understanding context, not just copying actions
